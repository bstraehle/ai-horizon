Privacy

AI HORIZON processes information when you play the game, when the app performs an end-of-run mission debrief, and when you submit a leaderboard score.

Offline mode

In offline mode, mission debrief is deterministic and leaderboard data will be stored in your browser's local storage on your device.

Online mode

In online mode, mission debrief uses AI and leaderboard data will be stored in the cloud. The app sends the following information to backend services:

  • Gameplay data, such as date, timer, score, stars collected, asteroids encountered or destroyed, and shots fired accuracy
  • Device-context data, such as user agent, viewport size, locale, time zone, and platform
  • 3-letter initials that you choose to submit to the leaderboard

How data is used

  • To generate an end-of-run mission debrief
  • To show and persist leaderboard rankings
  • To operate, secure, and monitor the app

Changes

This policy may be updated as the app or its backend services change. The current version will be posted at this page.
