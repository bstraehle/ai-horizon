AI HORIZON

A fast, arcade-style space shooter — collect stars, blast asteroids, and beat the clock.

How to play

Your goal is to score as many points as possible before the timer runs out. Move your ship to collect glowing stars and shoot incoming asteroids. Some asteroids are indestructible and take many hits. The game lasts one minute per run.

Controls

Move: Arrow keys or WASD — or point/touch the canvas to have the ship smoothly follow the cursor.

Fire: Spacebar, mouse button, or touch and hold — hold to continuously fire (rate-limited).

Pause / Resume: Esc (or the Pause button on-screen).

Scoring

Points are awarded for:

  • Regular asteroid: +10 points
  • Indestructible asteroid: +100 points
  • Regular star: +20 points
  • Bonus star: +50 points

The game tracks high scores and displays a small leaderboard at the end of each run.

Tips

  • Prioritize bonus stars when you see them — they give the biggest score boost.
  • Hold the fire button for continuous shooting, but note shots are rate-limited to prevent spamming.
  • Use mouse or touch for precise positioning; keyboard gives full directional control when you prefer it.
  • Aim for tight clusters of asteroids to get more points quickly, but avoid collisions — indestructible asteroids block bullets and take many hits.
  • Watch the timer — each run is short, so focus on high-value targets early.
