Scoring

A green high score indicates online mode (multiple players), red offline (single player).

  • Regular asteroid: +10
  • Hardened asteroid (10 hits): +100
  • Regular star: +20
  • Bonus star: +50
  • Every 1000 points: +250

If you score in the top 25, you may enter your initials to be featured on the leaderboard.
